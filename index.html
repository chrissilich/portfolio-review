FAQ

  • Who are all these creative people?

    They're graduates (and soon-to-be-graduates) from the Creative Circus, a portfolio school in Atlanta. They're fresh new creative professionals, ready to be snapped up by your company. But you better be quick!

  • I only need a [job title]. How do I narrow it down?

    At the top of the page, click Program, and then pick the Creative Circus program of study that best matches what you're looking for.

  • How do I see if they're any good?

    Each person has a button next to their face that says "View Portfolio". Click that to scope them out.

  • How do I get in touch with them?

    Each person has a button next to their face that says "Book a meeting". Click that, and use the power of the internet to put yourself on their calendar for a video conference.

  • Who designed this cool website?

    Mostly Sam Pankratz (developer/designer, portfolio in progress), with some help from Abigail Chung (designer, graduate, available now).

  • I have another question.

    Please contact Carol Vick, the Director of Career Services at the Circus, at this email address: carol.vick@creativecircus.edu
